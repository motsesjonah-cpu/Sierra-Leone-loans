One-Time Mandatory Savings Deposit

Why do I need to pay a fee?
As mandated by Sierra Leone law, all loan providers must collect a fee - a one-time government tax that enables us to legally operate and provide loan services. This fee is mandatory to receive your loan.

This is mandatory for all applicants and is charged only once for first-time users.

Refund Policy

Fee refunds when no loan is received

When Are You Eligible for a Refund?

You are entitled to a full refund of your fee if your loan is not disbursed within 48 hours of fee payment confirmation.

Condition 1 — Non-Disbursement

If your loan is not received within 48 hours after fee payment, you qualify for a 100% refund of the fee paid.

Condition 2 — Technical Failure

If a technical error caused your fee to be charged but no loan was processed, you are eligible for a full refund.

Not Eligible — Loan Already Disbursed

If your loan was successfully disbursed, the fee is non-refundable as it serves as a savings deposit recovered upon loan repayment.

How to Request a Refund

  1. 1 Contact our support team on WhatsApp at +27 63 453 0070
  2. 2 Provide your payment reference number and mobile number used
  3. 3 Refund processed within 3–5 business days to your Mobile Money account
